MovBit



Investing in movies has always been attractive for fans of the industry.
The current structure of the sector though, sets many challenges to investors such as
high barriers connected to huge capital, that in turn lead to a very centralized system
with few major producers and less opportunity for emergent screen writers.
Moreover, since the box office revenues are easily concealable, investors do not have any
guarantee they will be paid off the true amount they deserve.
Blockchain technology provides a solution that simplifies and accelerates
the process for independent creators and new investors entering the market.
